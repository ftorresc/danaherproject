The impossible goal of seeking perfection in Jiu jitsu performance is a fine metaphor for our lives in general. We all know we will stumble often and come up short at the end, but just as no matter how fast we ran as children, the rainbow always stayed well ahead of us, that didn’t make it any less alluring or captivating; so to on the mats. Whatever happened in your day, vexations, frustrations and disappointments, every training session is a chance to forget it all for a time and chase that impossible but fascinating goal. Keep yearning and keep learning, you’ll never be perfect but you can perfectly imperfect.
