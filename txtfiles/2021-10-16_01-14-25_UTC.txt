Got a good position? Get a better one! Don’t be satisfied with good when great is readily available. There are lots of good positions out there, but only a few great ones. Very often the transition from good to great is quite easy. Here, Garry Tonon has a good position - front head lock - but you can see his mind is plotting for better - the back! Nothings beats getting behind an opponent. Next time you’re doing well, ask yourself if you could be doing still better - usually the answer the yes. Don’t hesitate - take that next step and turn good into great
